Adding Edges into Graphs

Directed Graphs

For Graph G=(V,E)G = (V,E)G=(V,E), we now add one edge from uuu to vvv to make it as G′=(V′,E′)G' = (V', E')G′=(V′,E′). Now consider:

  • If uuu and vvv are mutually connected in GGG:
    • Nothing Changes!
    • Or we can say: ∀w∈V,[w]_conn(G)=[w]_conn(G′)\forall w \in V, [w]\_{conn(G)} = [w]\_{conn(G')}∀w∈V,[w]_conn(G)=[w]_conn(G′)
  • If uuu is not connected to vvv and vvv is connected to uuu:
    • The equivalent class of uuu and vvv is unioned!
    • [u]_conn(G′)=[v]_conn(G′)=w∈V∣u is reachable from w and w is reachable from v[u]\_{conn(G')} = [v]\_{conn(G')} = \\{ w\in V| u \text{ is reachable from } w \text{ and } w \text{ is reachable from } v\\}[u]_conn(G′)=[v]_conn(G′)=w∈V∣u is reachable from w and w is reachable from v
    • 相当于双向可达，即两个强联通块拼在一起。不过肯定不止于此，只要满足单向可达就行。
  • uuu is not reachable from vvv.
    • ∀w∈V,[w]_conn(G)=[w]_conn(G′)\forall w \in V, [w]\_{conn(G)} = [w]\_{conn(G')}∀w∈V,[w]_conn(G)=[w]_conn(G′)
    • 这个证明很经典

Trees in Graph

非空联通没有回路的无向图。

我们这里定义的是无根树，和数据结构中的有根树存在一定的差别。

  • Adding an edge into a tree, there will be a simple circuit.
  • Removing an edge, the tree will be unconnected.

leaf

A vertex is leaf when the degree of this vertex is 1.

  • If a tree has at least two vertices, then it will have at least 2 leaf.
  • A tree has nnn vertices has n−1n-1n−1 edges.

因此，树在图中具有非常良好的性质：

  • We define the tree T=(V,ET)T = (V, E_T)T=(V,ET​) is a subgraph of graph G=(V,E)G = (V, E)G=(V,E):
    • We suppose G is a connected and undirected graph. (Ensure ∃T\exists T∃T)
    • ET⊆EE_T \subseteq EET​⊆E
    • TTT is the maximum graph which has no circles for all the subgraphs in GGG.
    • TTT is the minimum connected graph for all the subgraphs in GGG
    • TTT may not be the only tree for graph GGG.
